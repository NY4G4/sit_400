Terms and Conditions

Welcome to Clars Vehicles Limited. By registering and using our platform, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

1. User Registration

- Users must provide valid and accurate information during registration.
- Vehicle owners must submit valid ownership documents and insurance details.
- Hirers must be at least **18 years old** and possess a **valid driver's license**.
- Accounts are linked to national ID numbers and cannot be duplicated.
- Users must keep their login credentials confidential.

2. Vehicle Registration and Listing

- Vehicle owners must ensure that their vehicles meet the **required safety standards** before listing.
- Vehicles will be subject to **inspection** before approval.
- Owners must keep vehicle insurance up to date. **Expired insurance** will lead to automatic listing suspension.
- Owners can deactivate their vehicle listings anytime but must honor existing bookings.
- Owners are responsible for ensuring that their vehicle details (e.g., model, condition) are **accurate**.

3. Hiring and Vehicle Usage

- Hirers must pick up and return vehicles at the designated **garage location**.
- Hirers are responsible for returning vehicles in **good condition**.
- Vehicles must be returned **on or before the agreed rental period** to avoid fines.
- A **10-minute grace period** is allowed for late returns before fines apply.
- Hirers must refuel and clean the vehicle before returning. Failure to do so **will incur extra charges**.

4. Late Returns and Fines

- Late returns beyond the 10-minute grace period will incur **hourly late fees**.
- Vehicles returned with damage, missing parts, or excessive dirt will result in **additional charges**.
- If a vehicle is not returned within **48 hours**, Clars Vehicles Limited will **take legal action**.
- Hirers who frequently return vehicles late or with damages may be **suspended from the platform**.

5. Cancellation Policy

- Hirers can cancel bookings **24 hours before the start time** for a full refund.
- Cancellations within 24 hours will result in a **cancellation fee**.
- If a booked vehicle becomes unavailable, Clars Vehicles Limited will **offer a replacement vehicle** or a full refund.
- Frequent last-minute cancellations may result in **temporary suspension**.

6. Payments and Refunds

- Payments must be made before a vehicle is hired.
- Refunds will be processed **before the scheduled hire time** if applicable.
- Delayed refunds will entitle the hirer to a **discount on their next booking**.
- Vehicle owners will receive **earnings directly to their registered bank accounts**.
- Subscription fees for vehicle owners depend on **vehicle type, age, and seat capacity**.

7. Damage and Liability

- Hirers are responsible for **any damages incurred** during the rental period.
- Insurance companies will handle **accident-related damages** where applicable.
- Severe damages may lead to **hirer suspension** or additional fines.
- If a hirer is found to be at fault, they may be required to **pay for repairs out-of-pocket**.
- Vehicle owners can request compensation if **their vehicles are damaged** during rental.

8. Privacy and Data Protection

- User data (e.g., ID numbers, payment details) is stored **securely** and will not be shared without consent.
- Clars Vehicles Limited will **notify users** of updates related to bookings, insurance, and account security.
- Feedback and ratings may be reviewed before being made **publicly visible**.
- Users have the right to request **data deletion** if they wish to close their accounts.

9. Account Suspension and Termination

- Clars Vehicles Limited reserves the right to **suspend or terminate accounts** if:
 1. False information is provided during registration.
 2. A user frequently cancels bookings without valid reasons.
 3. A hirer repeatedly returns vehicles late or with damages.
 4. A vehicle owner lists vehicles with **fraudulent** information.
- Suspended users may appeal by **contacting support**.

10. Amendments to Terms

- Clars Vehicles Limited reserves the right to update these terms at any time.
- Users will receive notifications about **major policy updates**.
- Continued use of the platform after updates constitutes **acceptance of new terms**.
